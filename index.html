Yenkism

Yenkism is an ancient polytheistic religion recently revived by Crafteri Industries. Rediscovered in ancient texts, it emphasizes balance, unity, and the pursuit of knowledge. Worshiping multiple deities, Yenkism connects followers to a profound spiritual tradition dating back thousands of years.

Yenkism teaches maintaining a balance between opposing forces and encourages the pursuit of wisdom through meditation, rituals, and community service. The pantheon includes gods like Aean, the King of Gods, and other deities representing light, darkness, nature, and elements. By reviving Yenkism, Crafteri Industries aims to share its timeless wisdom and values with the modern world.
